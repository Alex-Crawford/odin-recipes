Philly Steak

Description

The philly steak pizza, a philly cheesesteak, but a pizza

Ingredients

  • Green peppers
  • Onions
  • Philly steak
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Hand toss dough to according size
  2. Spread sauce
  3. Spread cheese(mozzarella)
  4. Spread veggies
  5. Spread steak
  6. Spread small amount of cheddar cheese
  7. Bake pizza at 550 degrees for approximately 5 minutes
  8. Rotate pizza 180 degrees and bake for an additional 5 minutes
  9. Remove from oven, cut, and serve
