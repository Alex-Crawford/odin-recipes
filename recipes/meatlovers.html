Meat Lovers

Description

The meat lovers features an assortment of meats

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Meatballs
  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Diced Ham

Steps

  1. Hand toss dough to according size
  2. Spread sauce
  3. Spread cheese
  4. Spread toppings starting with pepperoni
  5. Bake pizza at 550 degrees for approximately 5 minutes
  6. Rotate pizza 180 degrees and bake for an additional 5 minutes
  7. Remove from oven, cut, and serve
