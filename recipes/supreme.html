Supreme

Description

The supreme features a plethora of meats and vegetables

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Green Peppers
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Tomatoes
  • Italian Sausage
  • Black Olives
  • Diced Ham

Steps

  1. Hand toss dough to according size
  2. Spread sauce
  3. spread cheese
  4. Spread toppings from largest to smallest(i.e. pepperoni > diced ham)
  5. Bake pizza at 550 degrees for approximately 5 minutes
  6. Rotate pizza 180 degrees and bake for an additional 5 minutes
  7. Remove from oven, cut, and serve
